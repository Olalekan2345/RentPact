# RentPact — 3-Minute Demo Script

Goal: show the core pitch (escrow, gasless, dispute protection) and prove
the frequency feature is real — not just a monthly assumption with a label
change — by walking through both a **monthly** and a **yearly** lease.

Run `npm run dev` and have two browser profiles/incognito windows ready
(one for the tenant, one for the landlord) so you can switch without
signing in and out on camera.

---

### 0:00 – 0:20 — Landing (the pitch)

- Open `/`. Let the hero sit for a beat: *"Rent held in escrow, released
  on schedule, frozen on dispute."*
- Scroll to the escrow timeline preview. Click through **Monthly →
  Quarterly → Yearly** in the segmented control — call out that this is
  the *same component* rendering 12 compact nodes, 4 medium cards, or 2
  large cards, driven entirely by props.
- Scroll past "How it works" to the trust strip: *"Built on Arc · Powered
  by Circle · Gasless."*

### 0:20 – 0:50 — Sign up, monthly lease (tenant side)

- Click **Get started** → enter an email → narrate: *"No password, no
  seed phrase — a secure wallet is created silently behind this email."*
- Land on the empty dashboard — call out the designed empty state, not a
  blank screen.
- **New lease**: fill in a property address, pick **Monthly**, set
  450 USDC × 12 periods. Watch the live summary update as you type:
  *"You will deposit 5,400 USDC total. Your landlord receives 450 USDC
  every month for 12 periods."*

### 0:50 – 1:20 — Fund escrow (gasless + CCTP)

- On the deposit screen, show the unified Gateway balance across chains
  and the CCTP source-chain selector — narrate: *"I could fund this from
  Base Sepolia or Ethereum Sepolia; CCTP bridges it into Arc."*
- Click **Deposit into escrow**. Call out the **Gasless** badge — the
  tenant never touches native gas.
- Let the gold coin-drop animation play. Point at the transaction hash.

### 1:20 – 1:45 — Landlord signs (monthly lease)

- Switch to the landlord browser profile, open the invite link
  (`/leases/[id]/invite`).
- Show the full terms card — frequency, periods, total escrowed, sign-by
  deadline.
- Click **Sign lease** — one-click, gasless. Watch the handshake
  confirmation, then land on the lease detail page with the 12-node
  monthly escrow timeline.

### 1:45 – 2:15 — Second lease, yearly frequency (the payoff)

- Back on the tenant side, create a **second** lease on a different
  property — same flow, but this time pick **Yearly**, 2 periods.
- Narrate while filling it in: *"Same contract, same UI, completely
  different cadence — a 2-year lease with one release per year."*
- Fund it, sign it as the landlord, and land on its lease detail page.
- **Side-by-side moment**: flip back to the first (monthly) lease's tab
  and compare the timelines — 12 small nodes vs. 2 large yearly cards,
  both generated by the same `EscrowTimeline` component.

### 2:15 – 2:45 — Dispute freeze (the protection)

- On the monthly lease (as tenant), click **Raise dispute**. Show the
  weighty confirmation modal — the consequence explanation, not a casual
  action.
- Submit a reason. Show the timeline node icing over with the frost
  effect and the pulsing terracotta state.
- Open the dispute panel: reason, 7-day resolution countdown, frozen
  amount.
- Resolve it (as arbiter) in the tenant's favor — show the refund landing
  and the lease terminating cleanly.

### 2:45 – 3:00 — Close

- Back to the landlord dashboard: cumulative received count-up animation,
  next incoming release date.
- Closing line: *"Rent held in escrow, released on schedule, frozen on
  dispute — for any lease term, on any schedule, gasless, on Arc."*

---

**If recording time is tight**, cut the second (yearly) lease creation
short — reuse the pre-filled form fields, just swap the frequency segment
and periods before submitting, since the live summary card updating is
the moment that sells the feature, not the typing.
